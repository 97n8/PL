The PublicLogic Ecosystem

PublicLogic™

Parent brand and architectural backbone for lawful automation. Defines compliance, workflows, retention, continuity, and data standards. Operates in Microsoft 365 and CivicPlus; can run on the Polimorphic platform when speed matters.

PublicInsight™

Human systems and research. The cognitive and credentialed layer that ensures systems are designed with empathy, behavioral readiness, and professional credibility. Focused on staff experience and community trust.

PublicInstinct™

Partnership pilots and grants. Now in R&D. Explores performance behavior and civic instinct signals with ethical guardrails and municipal control.

PublicPulse™

Civic feedback and analytics. Measures public trust, staff wellbeing, and service performance so modernization stays grounded in human impact.

The VAULT™ Framework Family

The lawful operating system for cities and towns. VAULT converts statutory duties into digital frameworks that protect compliance, reduce burnout, and deliver measurable results. Each module can be deployed individually or combined as a unified civic system.

Partnership & Deployment

Technology & Implementation

Polimorphic hosts resident experiences and automations under the PublicLogic framework. Implementation partners deliver local setup and training. Strategic integrators can bundle VAULT for regional programs.

Vendor-Agnostic by Design

PublicLogic is vendor-agnostic. Adding Polimorphic improves speed and usability; it does not create dependency. Workflows remain portable and under municipal control.

Vendor Independence Clause

For clarity, neither party’s participation is dependent on any other vendor. PublicLogic may perform Service-Only Implementation Support without Polimorphic. Polimorphic may operate its platform without PublicLogic. Municipal clients retain permanent custody of their data and workflows.
