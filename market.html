3. Environmental + Public Health Extensions

VAULTMS4™ — Stormwater Compliance Automation

  • Auto-scheduled inspections for catch basins, outfalls, and BMPs
  • GIS dashboard linking each inspection to map coordinates
  • Form-to-report compiler for EPA-ready annual reports
  • Cross-link to VAULTFINANCE for capital budgeting
  • Outcome: ~60% reduction in spreadsheets and no missed NPDES deadlines

VAULTPFAS™ — Contaminant & Asset Tracking

  • Lab intake that ingests CSV/PDF results into structured records
  • Threshold alerts when PFAS levels exceed MCL
  • Resident notice generator (required under 310 CMR 22.07G)
  • Lifecycle linkage to VAULTFIX (assets) and VAULTFINANCE (grants)
  • Outcome: Single source of truth for environmental health compliance
